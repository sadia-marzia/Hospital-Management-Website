A Gynecologist is a doctor who specializes in the medical care of women and their reproductive systems, confirms the pregnancy and deals with mammograms, pap smear, uterine or vaginal infections, fertility problems or contraception, tubal ligations and hysterectomies.An Obstetrician is a doctor who specializes in the surgical care of women and their children during pregnancy, delivery/childbirth and post-natal care.Gynecologists offer preventive reproductive health care and treat diseases while obstetricians deliver babies and care for mother and baby during post-delivery.Gynecology deals with the female reproductive organs and Obstetrics deals with the care of women and their offsprings during pregnancy, childbirth and for a period of time following birth.

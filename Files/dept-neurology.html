Neurology

Neurology is the branch of medicine concerned with the study and treatment of disorders of the nervous system. The nervous system is a complex, sophisticated system that regulates and coordinates body activities.

The Neurology department of Labaid Hospital provides treatment for people with neurological disorders including diseases of the brain, spinal cord, peripheral nerves and muscles. The Department is committed in integrating its medical expertise for providing advanced Neurological care and treatment for our Patients. The department uses the latest technology and facilities, and is staffed by an experienced team of neurologists, nurses and technologists, who are committed to patient care.

Pediatrics (also spelled Paediatrics) is a branch of medicine that deals with the medical care of infants, children and adolescents. This department comprises of very prominent & distinguished physicians who are dedicated to the health and well-being of infants, children, adolescents and young adults.

A Pediatrician is a child’s physician who provides preventive health maintenance for healthy children, medical care for children who are acutely ill.Pediatricians manage the physical, mental and emotional well-being of their patients in every stage of their development – to reduce infant mortality, control infectious diseases and ease the difficulties of children and adolescents with chronic condition.

Based on your child’s unique needs, our team of specialists and nurses treat and care for every child as an individual.


Safeguarding your children’s health is our utmost priority. Our paediatricians are sensitive to these young patients’ needs and responsive to all your concerns – providing your family confidence and reassurance through your child’s journey to better health.
